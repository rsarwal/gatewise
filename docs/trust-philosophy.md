# Trust Philosophy

TrustLayer does not attempt to determine truth.

TrustLayer estimates confidence.

The goal is not to decide whether data is correct.

The goal is to help humans identify which records deserve attention before they enter operational systems.

Trust scores are based on observable evidence.

Every trust recommendation must include supporting evidence.

Every low-confidence recommendation must explain why human review is required.

TrustLayer follows a human-in-the-loop approach.

Humans remain the final decision makers.
